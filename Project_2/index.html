Interestingly, for all region, the population of using safe sanitization has been increasing throughout the years except for Oceania. For region Oceania , it is also increasing gradually after year 2001, year 2000 still has the highest population, which is 64.09%.

Mortality Rate of Top Country attributed to unsafe WASH Per 100,000 Population

*One emoji represents 10 deaths.

Chad, is known as the country with the highest mortality rate attributable to unsafe WASH. For male, there is a mortality rate of 100 out of 100,000. This also means that there will be 100 deaths out of the 100,000 population. While for female, the mortality rate is 90 per 100,000 population.
